How Our Pricing Works

It’s simple: if we don’t save you money on your mortgage’s origination fees or secure additional lender credits, you pay nothing. If we do save you money, our fee is 10% of whatever you save. We only charge you after you’ve closed on the loan and confirmed your final loan terms.
